Retrofit a wired doorbell to add WiFi and make it smart

Features

  • Send a notification on mobile phone when the door bell rings
  • Turn off door bell based on time of the day E.g. sleeping
  • Turn off door bell sound manually
  • Turn on a light signal when the door bell rings and the sound is off E.g. in a Zoom call
